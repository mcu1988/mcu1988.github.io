误差递推公式

文章神经网络原理推导中给出了神经网络误差反向传播的公式，

$$\eqalign{ & {\delta ^L} = {\text{(}}{{\text{a}}^L}{\text{ - y)}} \odot \sigma '({z^L}){\delta ^L} = {\text{(}}{{\text{a}}^L}{\text{ - y)}} \odot \sigma '({z^L}) \cr & {\delta ^l}{\text{ = }}({(\omega _{}^{l + 1})^T}{\delta _{}}^{l + 1}) \odot \sigma '({z^l}) \cr & \frac{{\partial C}}{{\partial {b_j}^l}}{\text{ = }}{\delta _j}^L \cr & \frac{{\partial C}}{{\partial {\omega _{kj}}^l}}{\text{ = }}{\delta _j}^l{a_k}^{l - 1} \cr} $$

上式中${a_j}^l$为输出层激活函数输出，${y_j}$为样本真值，$\sigma ({z_j}^l) = {a_j}^l$,对于输出层误差为

$${\delta _j}^l = ({a_j}^l - {y_j}){a_j}^l(1 - {a_j}^l)$$

对于输入层，误差为：

$${\delta _j}^l{\text{ = }}{z_j}^l(1 - {z_j}^l)\sum\limits_k^{} {\omega _{jk}^{l + 1}{\delta _k}^{l + 1}} $$

参数w和b的更新公式为

$$\eqalign{ & \Delta {b_j}^l{\text{ = }}{\delta _j}^l \cr & {{b'}_j}^l = {b_j}^l - \eta \Delta {b_j}^l \cr} $$ $$\eqalign{ & \Delta {w_{kj}}^l{\text{ = }}{a_k}^{l - 1}{\delta _j}^l \cr & {{w'}_{kj}}^l = {w_{kj}}^l - \eta \Delta {w_{kj}}^l \cr} $$
